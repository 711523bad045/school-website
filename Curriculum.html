Our Curriculum

Balanced, Innovative, and Future-Ready Education

Academic Structure

Our curriculum is designed to develop intellectual abilities, creativity, leadership, and moral values. Sanskriti School follows a structured, yet flexible curriculum based on national education standards (CBSE / ICSE / State Board as applicable).

Grades I - V (Primary School)

  • Focus on foundational literacy and numeracy
  • Interactive learning through stories, art, music, and play
  • Subjects: English, Hindi, Mathematics, Environmental Science, Computer Basics

Grades VI - VIII (Middle School)

  • Conceptual learning in core subjects
  • Introduction to formal science and social studies
  • Subjects: English, Hindi, Maths, Science, Social Studies, Computer Science

Grades IX - XII (Senior School)

  • Stream-specific curriculum (Science, Commerce, Humanities)
  • Emphasis on critical thinking, projects, and research
  • Preparation for board exams and competitive exams

Beyond Academics

We believe in all-round development through:

  • Art, Music, Dance, and Theatre
  • Physical Education and Sports
  • Life Skills and Value Education
  • Clubs and Competitions
